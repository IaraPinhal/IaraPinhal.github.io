About

My name is Iara, I’m in my "rollin 20’s". At the time I'm working at the Disney store Portugal (a "happy place" in the world) and studying at Etic. because the future "starts right here and right now" in order to be a designer, "I won't throw away my shot". I’m a young designer, "I know that I’m Naive", who had the privilege to work with some well known in the area, to have had some works exhibited (Sagres, Yorn, El Corte Inglés, Natura). Being a designer, the things I love the most are, "adding a few new paintings to my gallery", making illustrations, and doing photomontage.

I’m a person whose main objective is to touch "all the people" "hearts, and nothing can capture a heart" as Design can. I believe the best way to do this is, "watching all the world keep spinning round" and express what I see, the best way possible, the good and the bad things, these feelings are what make people "think of" what they need, to change to be, the best person possible.
